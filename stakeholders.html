Virtual Reality Galaxy Map

A new way to explore


The Stakeholders

There are diverse groups of stakeholders who could have vested interests in Virtual Reality Galaxy Map application. Those include:

  • Consumers
  • Astronomers
  • Virtual reality enthusiasts
  • Teachers
  • Designers and developers
  • Testers
  • Software Engineers
  • Client
  • Marketers
  • Sponsors

Key Stakeholders

The following sections cover the key stakeholder groups that benefit from this application.

Consumers

Consumers include the general public. These people have a general knowledge of astronomy and virtual reality. This application would introduce an interest to either astronomy or virtual reality, and would allow a user to enjoy traversing the galaxy.

Astronomy Experts

Astronomy experts include any individual who has a deep knowledge of astronomy and the galaxy. The goal for this application would allow these individuals to see the galaxy in a unique perspective as they travel and see their favorite stars.

Virtual Reality Enthusiasts

Individuals who own a virtual reality set and know everything about the set fall under this category. This application would satisfy their goal for new and unique VR applications.

Teachers

This group includes anyone who teaches others about the galaxy. The use of this application for teachers would be to give a better perspective to students and to show the magnitude of our galaxy, which satisfies an astronomy teacher's goal.

Designers, Developers, and Testers

This category of stakeholders encompasses the software team that works on the application. The goal for designers, developers, and testers is to create a unique application, which for this project utilizes new virtual reality technology to allows passionate astronomers and technology enthusiasts to adventure the galaxy from the comfort of their homes.

Software Engineers

Software engineers include individuals who determine the feasibility of a software application and solve/document the problems. The goal for software engineers is to determine the best way to overcome the requirements set in section 3 below.

Client

The client is the organization that this project is designed for. The goal for the client organization is to create a high quality and user friendly application to expand their user base.

Marketing

The marking stakeholders include the marketing team for the client organization. Goals for marketing include a visual stimulating application that is appealing for multiple user groups.

Sponsors

Sponsors include the outside organizations that support the designer and client groups for this project. The goal for sponsors is to use the funds efficiently.


Personas

The following section will cover two personas that would benefit from this application.

Astronomy Teacher

The first user case that benefits with this project is Galileo Koperniak, a passionate teacher located around Cambridge, Massachusetts, in the United States of America.

Koperniakan is an teaches astronomy at Harvard University who has taught for over 15 years. Established in 1962, Harvard University has become an world renowned university that teaches 21,000 students yearly. Koperniakan is head of the department of astronomy locate at 60 Garden Street and 160 Concord Avenue (M Building), where he makes faculty decisions that affect the outcome of that is learnt in astronomy classes.

Koperniakan takes pride in extended learning after hours. He believes these extended hours give students more engagement, and hopes that it can build interest in astronomy. Extra laboratory workshops at night are the main source of engagement that students have, but these classes are highly restricted based on weather conditions and use of the observatory. Koperniak goal is to find a way to bypass this constraint and to allow his classes to be more engaging for students.

Koperniakan has minimal experience with virtual reality applications. After a colleague from work introduced him to virtual reality google maps, he saw the educational potential of VR. Using funding from the University, Koperniakan purchased one HTC VIVE and this product. He was astounded by how easy it was to use on the first try.


Virtual Reality Enthusiest

The second user case is Dennis Gates, a young and brilliant second year medical student living in Oxford, England. Gates goes to school at Oxford University, which was rated the top university 2016-2017 and accepts only 1 in 5 students per year. Gates and has a very high standing in all his classes, and is a part of many clubs and extra activities.

During summers, Gates loves to travel and has been on many adventures everywhere in Europe and America, as well as Africa and even Alaska. This interest in the world pushes him to always keep exploring.

Alongside traveling, Gates loves video games, especially virtual reality. During his spare time, he can be found plugged into his VR set playing the latest games and testing the limits of new VR applications. Some of his favorite games are Paranormal Activity VR, The Deep, Time Machine, Minecraft Hololens, and Google Maps VR.

This application would satisfy Gate’s enjoyment of traveling and exploring new places, as well as add to his massive collection of VR applications.
